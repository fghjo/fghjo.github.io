Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

What counts as a collaborator?

Anyone you invite to a project — viewers, commenters, and editors all count toward your plan limit on the Starter tier.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
